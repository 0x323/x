DMCA Policy

Last Updated: May 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Lotto Chain Decentralized Betting Web3 Social ("We", "Lotto Chain") respects intellectual property rights and is committed to protecting content on our Platform in accordance with international laws, including the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA), the Berne Convention, and the TRIPS Agreement. This policy outlines our copyright ownership and the procedures for addressing copyright infringement.

2. Copyright Ownership

All content on the Platform, including source code, designs, logos, text, smart contracts (e.g., on EVM multi chain), and other materials, is owned by Lotto Chain or its licensors. This content is protected under:

  • Berne Convention: Provides automatic protection for software and digital works.
  • DMCA: Governs copyright enforcement in the U.S. and is recognized globally.
  • TRIPS Agreement: Sets minimum international standards for copyright protection.

Users are strictly prohibited from copying, reproducing, modifying, or distributing any content without written permission from Lotto Chain.

3. User-Generated Content

If Users upload content (e.g., social posts or betting data), they affirm that they own the rights to such content or have obtained the necessary permissions. Lotto Chain is not liable for any copyright violations arising from user-submitted content.

4. Copyright Infringement Procedure

4.1. DMCA Notice

If you believe that content on our Platform infringes your copyright, please send a DMCA notice to dmca@lottochain.com including the following information:

  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed.
  • The location of the allegedly infringing content (e.g., URL or post ID).
  • Your contact information (name, address, email, phone number).
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner.
  • A statement that the information in the notice is accurate and made under penalty of perjury.
  • Your physical or electronic signature.

4.2. Review Process

We will review DMCA notices within 7 business days. If the notice is valid, we will remove or restrict access to the infringing content. The user who uploaded the content will be notified of the takedown.

4.3. Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notification to dmca@lottochain.com including:

  • Identification of the removed content and its prior location.
  • Reasons why the content does not infringe copyright.
  • Your contact information.
  • A statement consenting to the jurisdiction of a federal court in [e.g., Delaware, USA].
  • Your physical or electronic signature.

We will review the counter-notification within 10 business days and may restore the content if appropriate.

5. Copyright and Blockchain

Smart contracts and source code deployed on Base Sepolia are protected as software works. However, on-chain data (e.g., token transactions) is public and cannot be removed. Copyright violations related to smart contract code will be addressed under the DMCA.

6. Repeat Infringement

Users who repeatedly infringe copyright may be banned from the Platform in accordance with our access termination policy.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Copyright Policy to reflect changes in legal or operational requirements. Updates will be published on the Platform.
