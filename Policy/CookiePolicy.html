Cookie Policy

Last Updated: May 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Lotto Chain Decentralized Betting Web3 Social ("we", "Lotto Chain") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to optimize your experience on our Platform. This policy explains the types of cookies we use, their purposes, and your rights to manage them, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the European Union’s ePrivacy Directive, and other global privacy standards.

By using our Platform, you agree to our use of cookies as described in this policy, unless you choose to disable them through your browser settings or the consent popup.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you access the Platform. They allow us to recognize your device, remember your preferences, and analyze interactions to improve our services. Similar technologies, such as tracking pixels or local storage, are also used for the same purposes.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use cookies in the following categories:

Type of Cookie Purpose Duration Third Party
Essential Cookies Enable core functionalities such as Web3 wallet authentication and navigation. Session-based or up to 1 year No
Analytics Cookies Measure performance and user behavior through tools like Google Analytics. Up to 2 years Yes (Google)
Advertising Cookies Deliver relevant ads via partners such as AdSense. Up to 18 months Yes (Google, Meta)
Functional Cookies Remember preferences such as language or display theme. Up to 1 year No

4. Purposes of Cookie Use

Cookies are used to:

  • Platform Operation: Ensure smooth Web3 wallet authentication, betting sessions, and navigation.
  • Performance Analysis: Collect data on visitor counts, session durations, and betting patterns for optimization.
  • Personalization: Customize content such as displaying betting history or social recommendations.
  • Advertising: Serve relevant ads based on your interests (where applicable).
  • Security: Detect suspicious activity, such as repeated login attempts.

5. Cookies and Blockchain

Cookies are not used to collect on-chain data (e.g., transactions on Base Sepolia), as such data is public and managed by smart contracts. However, cookies may store public wallet addresses to facilitate interaction with smart contracts.

6. Cookie Choices and Management

6.1. Consent Popup

In accordance with GDPR, we provide a cookie consent popup upon your first visit to the Platform. You may accept, reject, or customize your cookie preferences (excluding essential cookies, which are required for operation).

6.2. Browser Settings

You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may impact the functionality of the Platform, such as Web3 authentication. Guides for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari

6.3. Do Not Track (DNT)

Our Platform respects DNT signals if enabled in your browser, limiting tracking for analytics or advertising purposes.

7. Third Parties
